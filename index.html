Analysis

When comparing the Obesity Rate Vs. Poverty Rate, we can see that the lower income groups have a higher obesity rate. this may be due to the fact that the lower income group are unable to afford "Healthy Eating" habits.

The same goes for those who are earning less, have a higher percentage to have no healthcare at all. This raises the issue once again with affordability

However, when smoking habits are observed, again, the lower income earners tend to have a higher pecentage of smokers. Thus this creates a never ending cycle where the poor would not have great health at all due to bad choices.
